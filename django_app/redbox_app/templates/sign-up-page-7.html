Welcome to your Redbox!

Generative AI up to OFFICIAL-SENSITIVE

We're excited to have you join the community of early Redbox users! 🎉

Redbox is a Beta service that allows you to securely chat to an LLM with content up to OFFICIAL-SENSITIVE.

🌱 Being an early Redbox user

We need your help to make Redbox an effective tool for Civil Servants:

  • 💼 Use Redbox as much as possible - this will help us improve your experience
  • ⭐ Rate Redbox's responses - use the star rating at the bottom of each chat message
  • 💬 Send us detailed comments and feedback - post a message in the Redbox User Community (Google Chat)
  • 📋 Engage in surveys and research sessions - this helps us understand, in-depth, how Redbox is being used

🏗️ Redbox is in private beta

This means it is being actively developed and iterated. You might experience a bit of downtime or find that data, including documents, may be deleted unexpectedly.

🚀 Ready to get started?

Here are a few ideas to start your Redbox journey:

  • Write a response to an email
  • Draft a report
  • Analyse a policy document
  • Produce a summary report

⚠️ Mandatory safety warning

  • Please do not upload documents above OFFICIAL SENSITIVE. Redbox is not yet deployed at SECRET or TOP SECRET
  • Please do not upload anything containing personal data
