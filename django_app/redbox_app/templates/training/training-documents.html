Documents

You can attach documents to Redbox by pressing the Add file button.

You can also drag-and-drop files directly onto the page.

Once your files have uploaded you can write a prompt, asking questions of your uploaded documents. You do not need to upload your document to a chat more than once; Redbox keeps it for the remainder of the chat.
